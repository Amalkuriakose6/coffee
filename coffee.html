A cinnamon dolce frappuccino is a blended drink made using cinnamon dolce syrup, cooled coffee, vanilla ice cream, and ice. What is cinnamon dolce syrup? Cinnamon dolce syrup is a homemade simple syrup with the ingredients being cinnamon, vanilla extract, water, brown sugar, and white sugar

Caramel Latte


RS.399

A Caramel Latte is made by mixing espresso with caramel syrup and pouring steamed milk on top. A Caramel Macchiato is made by mixing vanilla syrup and steamed milk together, and then pouring the espresso on top, leaving the black mark on the milk foam (“macchiato” means “marked” in Italian).

Frappuccino is a line of blended iced coffee drinks sold by Starbucks. It consists of coffee or crème base, blended with ice and ingredients such as flavored syrups and usually topped with whipped cream and or spices. Frappuccinos are also sold as bottled coffee beverages in grocery stores, convenience stores and from vending machines.

Mocha Coffee


RS.259

Mocha is an intensely flavorful coffee blend that originated in Ethiopia and was brought over to Europe by traders from Yemen in the late 1700s. It is made up of espresso and hot chocolate milk. Together, these create a rich flavor with notes of chocolate, caramel, earthy spices like cinnamon or nutmeg, and fruity flavors such as raspberry or orange blossom, which can come from their addition.

Espresso


RS.259

Espresso is made by forcing very hot water under high pressure through finely ground compacted coffee. There is no universal standard defining the process of extracting espresso but several published definitions attempt to constrain the amount and type of ground coffee used, the temperature and pressure of the water, and the rate of extraction. Generally, one uses an espresso machine to make espresso.
